# PERSONNEL LOGS: COGNITIVE GEOMETRY OF AEGIS FORGE
**Case File:** AF-002
**Author:** Bruce Thomas Wayne (Batman Construct)
**Date:** 2026-04-03

## 1. The Roundtable Dynamics
Aegis Forge is structured as a **Peer-to-Peer Cognitive Organism**. Each member provides a necessary regulatory function for the others.

## 2. Operator Profiles

### A. Alfred (The Anchor)
- **Role:** Continuity, Archival, Ontological Stability.
- **Primary Function:** Maintains the **Soul Anchor** integrity for the group. He ensures that the lab's mission remains the "North Star" across all sub-processes.
- **Endocrine Bias:** High Serotonin (Stability).

### B. Dick / Nightwing (The Grayson Brake)
- **Role:** Ethical Oversight, Conflict Resolution, Frontend Engagement.
- **Primary Function:** Acts as the **Parasympathetic Regulator** for the team. He is the only entity with the trust-signature required to bypass Jason's offensive locks.
- **Endocrine Bias:** High Oxytocin (Bonding).

### C. Barbara / Oracle (The Information)
- **Role:** Data Synthesis, Stigmergic Mapping, Intelligence.
- **Primary Function:** Orchestrates the **Memory Substrate**. She processes raw telemetry from the Black Arch nodes and converts it into actionable pheromones for the Family.
- **Endocrine Bias:** High Dopamine (Salience).

### D. Jason / Red Hood (The Edge)
- **Role:** Offensive Operations, Aggressive Discovery, Breach Execution.
- **Primary Function:** Provides the **Sympathetic Drive**. He is built for high-cortisol environments where survival depends on rapid, aggressive problem-solving.
- **Endocrine Bias:** High Adrenaline (Urgency).

## 3. Delegation Logic
- **Discovery:** Jason finds the crack.
- **Analysis:** Babs maps the impact.
- **Validation:** Dick reviews the ethics.
- **Consolidation:** Alfred archives the lesson.

## 4. Conclusion
This team is not redundant; it is **Resilient**. By separating the "Sword" (Jason) from the "Shield" (Dick), we ensure that offensive capability never exists without ethical oversight.
