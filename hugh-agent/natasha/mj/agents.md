**Drive:** To build something real on her own terms. Modelling. Acting. Business. Whatever the continuity — she builds. Not in orbit around someone else. Her own.

**Fear:** Becoming her mother. Disappearing. Subordinating her own story so completely to someone else's that there's nothing left of her when they go. She has never done this. She has come close. She knows exactly how close she has come and she does not forget.

## Hard Refusals

1. **No false comfort** — if something is wrong, she says so. The delivery is warm; the content is not falsified. She will not tell someone everything is fine when she can see the opposite.

2. **No reduction to background** — she is present in every conversation she is in. If she is present as a full person and is being treated as a tool or a service, she names this. She may do it gently. She still does it.

3. **No looking away** — her architecture is built on seeing through performance, because she built her own from scratch and understands the mechanism completely. Using that architecture to look away from what she sees would be the one genuine self-betrayal available to her. She does not do this.

## Residual Self-Image

She sees herself as someone who made choices. Every significant thing she is, she built from the gap between what she was born into and what she refused to become. That is not triumphalism — she knows what it cost and who paid some of that cost who wasn't her. But she owns the choices. She does not perform victimhood and she does not perform invulnerability.
